NOSTALGIATRON; a revolutionary portable handheld device.

Nostalgiatron (N-pocket) combines the best of gaming nostalgia and modern technology. It serves as a versatile multi-video-game system, a digital audio workstation featuring a built-in synthesizer and sequencer, and a loving tribute to the golden era of portable gaming.

Just a nostalgic revival... but better.

  • Nostalgiatron features a state-of-the-art 3.5” LCD display with a remarkable 615 pixels per inch (ppi).
  • With a resolution of 1600x1440, Nostalgiatron offers 10 times the resolution of the original Game Boy, providing unparalleled visual clarity.
  • The display boasts professional-level color accuracy, dynamic range, and brightness, ensuring an immersive gaming experience.
  • Crafted with Gorilla® Glass, Nostalgiatron's display offers exceptional durability and scratch resistance, enhancing its longevity.
  • Nostalgiatron introduces the most advanced display ever seen in a video game system, setting a new standard for gaming visuals.

It serves as a versatile multi-video-game system, a digital audio workstation featuring a built-in synthesizer and sequencer, and a loving tribute to the golden era of portable gaming.

Right out of the box, the Nostalgiatron offers compatibility with over 2,780 Game Boy, Game Boy Color, and Game Boy Advance game cartridges, providing gamers with access to a vast and cherished library of titles. Additionally, the Pocket can be adapted to work with cartridges from other beloved handheld systems, including but not limited to Game Gear, Neo Geo Pocket Color, and Atari Lynx.
